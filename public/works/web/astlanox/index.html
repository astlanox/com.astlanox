About

It is a portfolio of my web site (this site). Not only the appearance and user experience but also the ease of operation has been considered. Sass and Pug were adopted in consideration of ease of operation and visibility. It also supports two languages, English and Japanese.

Concept

The concept is the universe that can be associated with my own name. We are consciously designed to remind you of the name Astlanox from logos and colors.
